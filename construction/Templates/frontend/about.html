Poliworks

Poliworks Inc. is a private corporation, PCAB licensed contractor, established in October 2012. The company began serving its customers through simple maintenance and minor facilities repair; then it slowly expanded by offering more extensive construction services adding interior repairs, renovation and project fit outs. Today, Poliworksis now capable to cater more clients. Being able to cater several industries; from commercial units, warehouses, medical laboratories and clinics, residential condo units and houses.

Putting Value on What's Important

The Company is all about flexibility; because we understand that every customer has their own ideas and preferences, we strive to incorporate these ideas and find the best possible outcome for each project, keeping in mind innovation and above all, quality results. We deliver cost effective solutions to fit any budget constraints.

Whether it is your dream house, corporate office expansion, facilities improvements, building restoration, or an entirely new structure, our team will be there to lend a hand in designing it, offering multiple solutions for your different needs. From the inception to commissioning, to actual fully operational facility, we will endeavor in not only meeting your requirements but exceed expectations. Our clients turn to us for our hands-on approach project management technique and knowledge-driven solutions for their complex construction projects. We are always here to build your ideas!
